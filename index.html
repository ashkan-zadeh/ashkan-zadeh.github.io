Ashkan Yousefi Zadeh

PhD Candidate
CARRS-Q, Queensland University of Technology

I am a PhD candidate at Queensland University of Technology, supervised by Dr. Xiaomeng Li, Prof. Andry Rakotonirainy, Prof. Ronald Schroeter, and Prof. Sebastien Glaser.

My research focuses on developing human-centric explanation models for automated vehicles, bridging the gap between AI decision-making and human understanding through natural language processing and explainable AI techniques.
